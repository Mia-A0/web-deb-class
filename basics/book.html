Me Before You

Jojo Moyes

Me Before You, by Jojo Moyes, tells the story of the connection between Louisa "Lou" Clark and Will Traynor. Lou is free-spirited, and lives in a small town. Will is wealthy, and once led an adventurous life, but became a quadriplegic after an accident. Lou is hired to be Will's caregiver. She tries to show him that his life is still worth living.

Jojo Moyes is a novelist and screenwriter. Her books include the bestsellers Me Before You, After You and Still Me, The One Plus One, The Giver of Stars, Someone Else's Shoes and her short story collection Paris for One and Other Stories. Jojo’s novels have been translated into forty-six languages, hit the number one spot in twelve countries, sold over fifty-seven million copies worldwide and been Reese Witherspoon and Richard and Judy Book Club picks. Me Before You has now sold over fifteen million copies worldwide and was adapted into a major film starring Sam Claflin and Emilia Clarke. Jojo is part of BBC Maestro’s online platform of world-class experts with her course, Writing Love Stories, which is available now. Jojo lives in the UK.
